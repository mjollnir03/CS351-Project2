Samsung 27" Monitor

The Samsung Odyssey G52A features a 27-inch QHD (2560 x 1440) resolution display, offering sharp visuals and extra screen space compared to Full HD resolution. With AMD FreeSync compatibility, the G52A helps reduce screen tearing and stuttering for a seamless gaming experience.
